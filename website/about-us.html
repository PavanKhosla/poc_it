Overview

Medical record keeping is an integral part of patient care monitoring in the modern world however, developing nations still have limited access to these services. With the recent boom in big data analysis and evidence-based medical treatment analysis, data stored in these servers can be used on a never before seen scale to further develop medical knowledge and improve patient outcomes all over the world. POC-IT offers a platform that not only integrates community health networks but also offers an intuitive website where providers can monitor and track their patientâ€™s perioperative care. POC-IT will soon also allow for real-time data trend analysis of patient outcomes based on factors such as geographic locations or if patients did or did not receive a specific treatment. Importantly, POC-IT is supported by a FireBase database that not only offers real-time cloud connectivity but also offers strong cybersecurity for protected health information through Google.
